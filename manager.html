Sync TaskRabbit Jobs

Add your TaskRabbit iCal feed to Google Calendar so TR jobs block your availability automatically:

  1. Open TaskRabbit → Account → Settings → Calendar Sync
  2. Copy your iCal URL
  3. Open Google Calendar → Other calendars (+) → From URL
  4. Paste the URL and click Add Calendar

Google syncs the feed every ~8 hours. TR jobs will automatically block client booking slots.
